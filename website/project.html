Saylani Welfare International Trust

Saylani Welfare International Trust has launched numerous projects aimed at improving the quality of life for underprivileged communities. These projects span multiple sectors, including food distribution, education, healthcare, water supply, financial aid, and disaster relief. With a focus on sustainability and long-term development, Saylani has transformed the lives of millions of people in Pakistan and around the world.

Food Distribution Project

The organization provides food to over 100,000 people daily across Pakistan through the following initiatives: Dastarkhwan (Public Feeding Program): Saylani sets up free meal centers in various cities, including Karachi, Lahore, and Islamabad. Daily Ration Distribution: Monthly grocery packs containing flour, rice, oil, sugar, lentils, and spices are provided to low-income families.
